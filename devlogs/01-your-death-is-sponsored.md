## We don't recruit heroes

We cast **disposable people**.

**The Crawler Cast: Your Death Is Sponsored** is an isometric 3D hack'n slash roguelite where you run an **"Actor" Agency** — in the TV sense of the word: broke, indebted, or just in the wrong place at the wrong time, sent into a **live-streamed dungeon** to entertain billions of viewers.

Survive? Sure, that's the stated goal.  
Enrich the agency? That's **audience ratings**.

The Showrunner doesn't talk to you. It talks to **chat**.  
You are the content.

---

## What sets us apart

### 1. Spectacle before survival

Killing isn't enough. You need to put on a show. Absurd risks raise **Hype**; playing safe annoys the audience.

### 2. Reality-TV satire

Raw dungeon brutality vs cheerful corporate packaging: jingles, confetti, product placements on monsters. B-movie gore + prime-time.

### 3. The 4th wall

Chat (Twitch or simulated AI) is the voyeur audience. The Showrunner addresses them. The contestant on set is just a product.

---

## Where we are (honestly)

Today, the playable prototype covers **core combat**:

- Circular arena with **enemy waves**
- Cursor-driven movement, **dash with invulnerability**
- Melee / ranged / skill attacks with **ground telegraphs**
- **Stats**, level-up, point allocation
- **Loot, inventory, equipment**, health and mana potions
- Full enemy AI: patrol → alert → chase → attack → flee
- Goblins and skeletons

What's next:

- **Hype** gauge and audience economy
- **In research:** live **Twitch** chat integration — audience reactions feeding the show (simulated chat in prototype)
- **Showrunners** with variable personalities (The Butcher, The Diva, The Accountant…)
- **Intermissions** — ad breaks, vending machines
- **Agency Office**: recruitment, casting, lobbying
- Themed runs across **15 floors** (kitsch medieval, wild west, lovecraft…)

We're not selling a finished game. We're building something ambitious — and we want you along for the ride.

## Visual world

Gritty low-poly, 80s/90s VHS aesthetic. The contestant stays **contemporary** (tracksuit, riveted plates, Disposable™ wristband) in any décor recycled by TV. Monsters are **mascot-horror**: body grotesque + corporate mascot + sponsor.

*Rippy Energy™* on a goblin? Normal.

| Color code | Role |
|------------|------|
| Holographic cyan | Corporation, Showrunner, cold tech |
| Orange / red / blood | Arena, violence, rust |
| Neon magenta | Ads, live feed, overconsumption |
