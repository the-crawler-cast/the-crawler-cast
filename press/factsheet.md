## Short description

Isometric 3D hack'n slash roguelite where death is a consumer product and the audience is always right. The player runs an "Actor" Agency and sends disposable people into a live-streamed dungeon. Survival matters; driving ratings is what pays.

---

## Long description

**The Crawler Cast: Your Death Is Sponsored** blends cursor-driven roguelite combat with cynical reality-TV satire. Inspired by *Smash TV*, *The Running Man*, and *Hades*, the game casts the player as a casting director for a deadly show: recruit contestants, send them into the arena, and maximize audience ratings — even at the cost of their lives.

The Showrunner, the show's sadistic AI, addresses chat (Twitch or simulated), not the contestants. Spectacle beats survival: absurd risks raise Hype; playing safe annoys the audience.

Gritty low-poly aesthetic, B-movie gore + prime-time confetti. "Mascot-horror" monsters with corporate sponsors.

## Features (current + roadmap)

**Prototype (internal):**
- Isometric 3D cursor-driven combat, dash, telegraphs
- Enemy waves, loot, inventory, equipment
- Stats, level-up, point allocation
- Full enemy AI

**Roadmap:**
- Hype gauge & audience economy
- **In research:** live chat integration (Twitch) — audience reactions feeding the show; simulated chat in prototype
- Showrunners with variable personalities
- "Ad break" intermissions, vending machines
- Agency Office (meta-progression)
- Themed 15-floor runs
- 2-player co-op (distinct economic interests)
